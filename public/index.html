About Me

Crafting digital
experiences that matter

I'm Zeeqo, a Full Stack Developer with a deep passion for building web applications that are not only functional but genuinely delightful to use. I bridge the gap between design and engineering, turning ideas into polished digital products.

My journey started with curiosity about how the web works — from a simple HTML page to complex, scalable systems. Over the years I've sharpened my skills across the entire stack, from pixel-perfect interfaces to robust server-side architecture.

I believe great software is invisible — it just works, feels fast, and gets out of the user's way. That philosophy drives every decision I make, from architecture choices to micro-interaction design.

When I'm not coding, I'm exploring new technologies, contributing to open source, and occasionally sketching UI concepts on paper.
